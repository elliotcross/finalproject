Music, Music, Music

In my free time, I'm a musician, songwriter, and entrepreneur. I play guitar, drums, vocals, bass, keyboards and piano. I like a wide range of musical styles including jazz, metal, progressive and classical. I write music that draws on progressive rock, jazz, metal, and classical influences. I'm currently lead guitarist and singer for the band Triacs. I'm also the songwriter. Triacs is a progressive rock band, with jazz, metal, and psychedelic rock influences. I also founded the record label Floss Records. From a recording studio in Bellingham, Washington, Floss Records provides recording and production services for Pacific Northwest artists. I've been performing and recording my original music since 2010, both solo and ensemble. My goal is to continue to create my own music and to expand the Floss Records label and music production business.

Bands

2019-2022: Triacs
Triacs is a Bellingham, Washington-based, Progressive Rock group. Credits: songwriting, guitarist, singer, live performances, and recording.
2016-2019: Sea
Sea was a Redmond-based band performing in the greater Seattle area. Its main genres were Grunge and Alt/Rock with Jazz influences. Credits: songwriting, drumming, live performances, and recording.
2015-2017: Effective Nuclear Charges
Effective Nuclear Charges was a live Grunge Rock cover band performing in Bellevue and Seattle, WA. Credits: Drummer and guitarist.
2012-2014: Solo artist
Solo performances and guest appearances, greater-Phoenix, Arizona. Songwriting, live performances, and recording.
