My name is Elliot Cross. I'm in my twenties, living in the Pacific Northwest region of the U.S. after decades of living all over the states and in a few countries overseas. I am studying web design at Bellevue College in Bellevue, Washington. I also work full time doing home appliance repair. And, I am a musician.

Studies

I've been taking classes in business, marketing, and web design at Bellevue College for two years. I've already had an introduction to HTML and CSS. This semester I am taking a class that covers JavaScript in addition to HTML and CSS. Ultimately I hope to put what I am learning in business, marketing, and website design to work running my own record label and promoting my own band.

Music

I am a guitarist and drummer, and I write music. I've been performing with bands in locations from Phoenix, Arizona to Bellingham, Washington for ten years. My current band, Triacs, based out of Bellingham, Washington, is a progressive and psychedelic rock band. I also have a recording studio located in Bellingham, where I record and produce music for my start-up independent record label, Floss Records.

Work

Besides working on school and music, I spend a lot of time at my job. I work doing mechanical repairs, tooling around western Washington State from Vancouver to Blaine in my repair van. I go out on assignment for a large national home appliance repair company. It's a lot of time on the road, but it keeps the roof over my head!
